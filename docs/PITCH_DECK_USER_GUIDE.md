# Pitch Deck Generator - User Guide

## What is it?

The AI Pitch Deck Generator creates professional, investor-ready pitch decks in minutes. Powered by Claude AI, it generates compelling content tailored to your business.

## Getting Started

### Step 1: Access the Generator

1. Log in to your Nanowork dashboard
2. Click **"PITCH DECK"** in the left sidebar (code: 05)
3. Or navigate to `/dashboard/pitch-deck`

### Step 2: Describe Your Business

Enter information about your business:

**Required:**
- **Business Description**: Explain what you're building, who it's for, and what makes you unique. Be specific about:
  - Your product or service
  - Target market and customers
  - Key features or benefits
  - Traction (users, revenue, growth)
  - Competition and your advantage

**Optional:**
- **Company Name**: Leave blank for AI suggestion
- **Tagline**: Leave blank for AI to generate
- **Target Raise**: E.g., "$2M Seed Round"

### Example Input

```
Building a B2B sales automation platform that helps teams close 
deals 3x faster with AI-powered insights and automated follow-ups. 
Target market is mid-size companies with 10-50 sales reps. 

Key features:
- Lead enrichment and scoring
- Automated outreach sequences  
- Deal likelihood predictions
- Integration with Salesforce and HubSpot

Traction: $100K MRR, 50 customers, 3 Fortune 500 companies, 
80% logo retention, 40% month-over-month growth.

Raising $3M seed to expand sales team and build integrations.
```

### Step 3: Generate Your Deck

1. Click **"Generate Pitch Deck"**
2. Wait 20-40 seconds while AI creates your deck
3. You'll get 10 professional slides:
   - Cover (title slide)
   - Problem (market pain)
   - Solution (your product)
   - Market Size (TAM/SAM/SOM)
   - Business Model (revenue)
   - Traction (metrics)
   - Competition (advantages)
   - Team (founders)
   - Financials (projections)
   - The Ask (funding)

## Editing Your Deck

### Navigate Between Slides

**Left Sidebar:**
- Click any slide thumbnail to jump to it
- Current slide is highlighted in white

**Navigation Buttons:**
- Use "Previous" and "Next" at the top
- Keyboard shortcuts coming soon

### Edit Content

**Edit Mode** (default):
- Click into any field to edit
- Change slide titles
- Rewrite main content
- Modify bullet points
- Updates save automatically

**Preview Mode**:
- Toggle to see presentation view
- Check how slides look
- Switch templates to compare styles

### Choose a Template

Four professional styles available:

**Y Combinator**
- Minimal, data-focused
- White background, black text
- Best for: Data-heavy, B2B pitches

**Sequoia**
- Storytelling narrative
- Dark gradient background
- Best for: Consumer, vision-driven

**Modern SaaS**
- Clean and contemporary
- Light gray, modern design
- Best for: Tech startups, SaaS

**Corporate**
- Formal and detailed
- White with borders
- Best for: Enterprise, traditional industries

## Using the AI Assistant

### Open the Assistant

Click **"AI Assistant"** button at the bottom right of the editor.

### Improve Any Slide

1. Navigate to the slide you want to improve
2. Open AI Assistant panel
3. Enter what you want:
   - "Make this more compelling"
   - "Add specific data and numbers"
   - "Rewrite for enterprise buyers"
   - "Simplify and make more concise"
   - "Emphasize competitive advantages"
4. Click **"Improve"**
5. Wait 5-10 seconds
6. Slide updates automatically

### Quick Actions

Pre-written improvements you can apply with one click:
- **Add more data**: Makes slide more specific and quantitative
- **Make more compelling**: Improves investor appeal
- **Simplify**: Makes content more concise

### Best Practices

✅ **Good instructions:**
- "Add specific revenue numbers and growth rate"
- "Rewrite problem statement to emphasize pain points"
- "Make market sizing more credible with sources"

❌ **Vague instructions:**
- "Make it better"
- "Fix this"
- "Improve"

## Exporting Your Deck

### PDF Export (Recommended)

1. Click **"Export PDF"** button (top right)
2. New window opens with formatted slides
3. Use browser's print function:
   - File → Print
   - Choose "Save as PDF"
   - Select landscape orientation
   - Save to your computer

**Tip**: For best quality, use Chrome or Edge browser.

### Markdown Export

Great for:
- Version control (Git)
- Import to Deckset or Marp
- Editing in text editors
- Sharing via GitHub

1. Hover over "Export PDF"
2. Click "Export as Markdown"
3. File downloads automatically

### JSON Export

Save complete deck data:
- All slide content
- Template choice
- Metadata

1. Hover over "Export PDF"
2. Click "Export as JSON"
3. File downloads automatically

**Future**: Import JSON to restore deck

## Pro Tips

### Writing Your Business Description

**Include:**
- What you're building (1 sentence)
- Who it's for (ICP)
- Key features (3-5)
- Why you're different (unique insight)
- Traction (real numbers)
- Current stage and funding goal

**Don't:**
- Use vague buzzwords
- Skip the specifics
- Oversell without data
- Ignore competition

### Getting Better Results

1. **Be specific**: "50 customers, $100K MRR" beats "growing fast"
2. **Include numbers**: AI incorporates real data into slides
3. **Mention competitors**: AI will position you against them
4. **State your advantage**: AI emphasizes your unique approach
5. **Provide context**: Industry, market stage, team background

### Refining Your Deck

**After generation:**
1. Read through all 10 slides
2. Identify weak or generic sections
3. Use AI Assistant to strengthen them
4. Add your own specific data
5. Customize for your audience
6. Switch templates to find best fit

### Before Sharing with Investors

**Checklist:**
- [ ] All numbers are accurate
- [ ] Team slide has real founder backgrounds
- [ ] Financial projections are realistic
- [ ] Problem statement resonates
- [ ] Solution is clear and specific
- [ ] Market sizing is defensible
- [ ] Competition section is honest
- [ ] The Ask matches your actual raise
- [ ] Contact info is correct
- [ ] Export looks good in PDF

## Troubleshooting

### Generation Failed
- Check your internet connection
- Ensure business description isn't empty
- Try refreshing the page
- If problem persists, contact support

### Slides Look Wrong
- Check template selection
- Try switching to different template
- Refresh the page
- Try different browser (Chrome recommended)

### Export Not Working
- Allow popups in your browser
- Try different export format (Markdown)
- Check browser console for errors
- Use Chrome or Edge for best compatibility

### AI Assistant Not Responding
- Check internet connection
- Make sure instruction isn't empty
- Try simpler instruction
- Wait a few seconds and retry

### Content Too Generic
- Provide more specific business description
- Use AI Assistant with specific instructions
- Edit manually with your own data
- Include real numbers in original input
